About Us


How We Met:

Dave and Kayla first met in December 2011, while they were students at McGill University. At that time, Dave was the President of the Management Undergraduate Society, which had an office in the concourse of the Bronfman building. Students organizing events often came and went from the office throughout the day for meetings, and Dave usually knew them all - except the day that Kayla visited the office with her friend Natalie (bridesmaid). She caught his eye immediately, and he introduced himself. Then, he had possibly the best idea of his life, one that would forever change both of their lives: he offered Kayla a chocolate. The rest, as they say, is history.

Our Story:

By the spring of 2012, Kayla and Dave had been dating a few months and things were going very well, but their summer plans were uncertain. Dave was graduating, and was not sure whether job opportunities would take him to another city, while Kayla still had over a year left to complete her degree. She had to decide whether to go back to her home town of Fonthill where she had a summer job lined up, or take a chance on her first summer in Montreal. They both decided to stay. By the end of that summer, Dave and Kayla knew they were deeply in love and told each other so. She came to Maine with him and his parents, and she brought him wine tasting in the Niagara peninsula. That summer was filled with laughter, new adventures and love.

Over the years, Kayla and Dave have shared many wonderful experiences. They travelled to Tokyo and Hong Kong to visit with Dave’s brother Rick (best man) and his family. They have gone sailing, visited friends’ cottages, attended music festivals, coached a softball team, seen New York City at warp speed, walked the winter wonderland of Quebec City, become Godparents to their niece Evelyn, and even been guests at eight weddings together. Their most fulfilling experience as a couple, however, has been raising their dog Beijo. Although their cat Boots was not too thrilled at first by his new roommate, the two brothers have learned to coexist peacefully and Dave and Kayla could not be happier.

Dave and Kayla have grown together through their shared experiences - whether it was living in a tiny apartment together that was sweltering hot in the summer, or dealing with the months of stress and sacrifice involved in Dave writing the three levels of the CFA exam, or supporting each other through the tragic loss of loved ones. It is in these moments that they learned each other’s true nature, and it forever strengthened their bond. Now, they look to build the next chapters of their life together – as husband and wife.

The Proposal:

On Kayla’s birthday, July 30th, Dave took her back to the restaurant where they had their first date. On their walk home, they stopped to enjoy the full moon and a cool evening breeze on a warm summer night. They sat at a park bench in Phillips Square and Dave told Kayla why he wanted to spend the rest of his life with her. He got down on one knee, popped the question, and she said yes!
